George

George, just like Marty, is a lady nanday conure.

I raised her since she was a very "smoll birb", as parrots memes say. It was 2002, and I wasn't even attending high school yet.

Now she's a grumpy (granny?) 18-years old conure lady. She absolutely loves scritches, sleeping, and eating her (very expensive) pellets.



Marty

Marty too is a lady nanday conure!

Marty is a very recent addition to our small flock. She lived most of her life, since her hatchday (February 2008), in a very unhealthy and dangerous environment, with a cat constantly trying to eat her.

Fortunately for her, the previous owner decided to get rid of her, and since we were looking for a mate for George, I happily took Marty in our home.

She has three fractured bones, preventing her from flying and walking correctly, a severe feather-plucking issue, and her temperament is unpredictable sometimes. But with meds, patience and perseverance, she's doing better every day.
